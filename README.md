# Collectibles Market Research

Price index analysis of luxury watch, sneaker, and sports card 
markets benchmarked against S&P 500 and Gold (2015–2025).

## Tools
Python · pandas · yfinance · matplotlib · openpyxl

## Markets Tracked
| Market | Key Assets |
|--------|-----------|
| Luxury Watches | Rolex Submariner, Rolex Daytona, AP Royal Oak |
| Sneakers | Air Jordan 1, Nike Dunk Low, Yeezy 350 |
| Sports Cards | LeBron James RC, Mike Trout RC, Luka Doncic RC |
| Benchmarks | S&P 500 (^GSPC), Gold (GC=F) via yfinance |

## Key Findings
- Sports Cards peaked at +1,474% in 2021 — sharpest COVID bubble
- Luxury Watches peaked at +219% in 2022, more gradual correction
- Sneakers peaked at +257% in 2022, fastest post-peak decline
- All collectibles showed high volatility vs S&P 500 and Gold
- Consistent with illiquidity premium theory in alternative assets
